Latitude vs. Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warner as one apporaches the equator (0 Deg. Latittude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This is due to the tilt of the earth which is the reason why have seasons of the year.
